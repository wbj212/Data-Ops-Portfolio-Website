The Strategic Business Case

In modern analytics, the bottleneck for high-performance modeling is rarely the algorithm, but the integrity of the underlying data. This dashboard monitors the 'Data-to-Model' pipeline, calculating a real-time Integrity Score that accounts for successful record ingestion and field-level null density. By visualizing the inverse correlation between data quality and Model MAPE (Mean Absolute Percentage Error), this tool enables Data Operations teams to proactively identify 'Schema Drift' in disparate sources—such as Google Ads, Meta, or BigQuery—before it compromises downstream business decisions.

Technical Methodology

  • Automated ETL logic utilizing SQL CTEs to flag record-level anomalies[cite: 29, 30].
  • Stochastic simulation of 'Privacy Strictness' to project impact on forecast accuracy[cite: 14].
  • Schema validation protocols to identify unexpected volatility in third-party datasets.

Systems Thinking Applied

Leveraging Chemical Engineering principles of "Mass Balance" to ensure data conservation across the pipeline—what enters the warehouse must be accounted for in the model.
